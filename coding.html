Coding Dictionary
Telework Telework (also known as telecommuting or remote work) describes the practice of employees working outside of the physical office campus. telework OR "work from home" OR "hybrid work" OR "flexible work arrangements"
Training Programs Career training (or professional development) describes educational programs for employees to enhance their skills and experience. "professional development" OR "job training" OR "training opportunities" OR "skill development"
Wellness Programs Wellness programs are designed to improve and promote health and fitness mentally and physically. "wellness" OR "employee wellness program" OR "healthy" OR "fitness" OR "Employee assistance program" OR EAP
Insurance Plans Insurance benefits include health, dental, eye, and other relevant medical needs. "health insurance" OR "medical coverage" OR "medical insurance"
Paid Time Off Paid time off is personal time that employees take to spend out of the office (and not working) while still receiving salary and benefits. "paid time off" OR "PTO" OR "paid holidays" OR "vacation" OR "time off accrual"
Tuition Assistance Tuition assistance programs give employees compensation for the past or future cost of education tutition. tuition OR "tuition reimbursement" OR "education reimbursement" OR "student debt" OR "student loans"
Transit Subsidies Transportation subsidies refer to employer benefit programs that reduce the cost of moving (including relocation assistance) or commuting to the workplace. relocation OR "moving costs" OR relocate OR "Free Bus Transportation" OR "transit pass" OR "transit expense"
